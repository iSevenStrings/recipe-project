Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound of dried chickpeas
  • 2 ounces of fresh cilantro
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground coriander seeds
  • 2 teaspoons of kosher salt
  • 2 cups of vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Soak chickpeas in cold water overnight.
  2. Combine chickpeas and seasonings in food processor and pulse until chickpeas are finely minced.
  3. Transfer mixture to a bowl and cover then refrigerate for 15 minutes. Then, use a table spoon to scoop out the mixture and form into balls.
  4. Cook the balls in vegetable oil that is heated to 375 degrees. Cook until browned on the bottom side and then flip.
  5. Serve with tahini or hummus with cucumber and tomato.
