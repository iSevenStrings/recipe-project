Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons of grated garlic
  • 2 teaspoons of grated ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cayenne
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground turmeric
  • 1 can of tomato sauce (15 ounces)
  • 1 can of full fat coconut milk
  • 2 cans of chickpeas (15 ounces each)
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 cups of baby spinach

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large pot on medium heat.
  2. Add the grated garlic and ginger. Stir constantly while cooking until fragrant.
  3. Add the cayenne and turmeric and stir for 30 seconds, then slowly pour in the tomato sauce.
  4. Add the chickpeas, then stir in the coconut milk.
  5. Add salt. Lower the heat and bring the mixture to a simmer.
  6. Cover with a lid and cook for 10 to 12 minutes.
  7. Add the fresh spinach. Turn off the heat and cover with lid. After the spinach has wilted, stir it into the curry.
  8. Serve with rice and naan. (optional)
